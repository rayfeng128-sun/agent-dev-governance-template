# Lightweight Change Template

## User Story

As a [role], when [scenario], I want [capability], so that [value].

## Goal

Describe the concrete outcome this lightweight change should achieve.

## Scope

- In scope: describe the files, pages, docs, or demo states affected.
- Out of scope: describe anything intentionally not changed.

## Prototype Source

Record the Obsidian or Stitch baseline for page-facing work. If this change does not affect a page, write: `No page prototype impact`.

## Story Boundary

Describe where this story starts and ends. State what follow-up steps or adjacent stories are intentionally out of scope.

## Impact

Describe the actual impact on frontend, backend, Mock/demo data, permissions, config, tests, and demo paths. Use `No impact` for areas not touched.

## Acceptance Criteria

- State the observable conditions that prove the story or story slice is complete.

## Verification

- Record the exact command or smoke check executed and the result.

## Risks

Record remaining risks, unconfirmed assumptions, or the condition that would require upgrading to the full closure flow.
